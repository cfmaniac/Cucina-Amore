Cucina & Amore began with Italian gourmet specialty foods and quickly grew beyond Italian borders. With an increased focus on unique, innovative products from around the world, we created Kitchen & Love for our new line of amazingly delicious, convenient and innovative products. The new Kitchen & Love includes our Quinoa and Farro quick meals with mouth-watering sauces, a new line of artisan preserves, and many more to come. Our Kitchen & Love products come in colorful packaging and will be available in the Fall of 2017.
