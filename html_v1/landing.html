INTERNATIONAL DELICACIES


International Delicacies is an entrepreneurial, family-owned novel food company based in the San Francisco Bay area.


As a marketer of fine foods, International Delicacies has been focusing on the innovation and distribution of high quality specialty products from around the globe since 1983. A combination of consumer-needs, our passion about food and our decades of experience is what inspires us today to create innovative, healthy foods through innovation and technology. It drives the way we create new products and moves us to hold ourselves to high standards of quality and doing things the right way. Because delicious and quality products are good for our bodies, communities and the worlds.

Our new Cucina & Amore and Kitchen & Love lines of products are based on a rich history of tradition and innovation.
